My Relationship with One Directions Music:

When One Direction first came out I was only about nine and already part of the wave of people that hated them. I would hear their songs on the radio and sing along until realizing it was One Direction and promptly shut up in disgust. But as my eight grade year rolled around I started getting really into their music opting not to give a crap about anybody elses opinions and fell in love with their music. When Zayn malik left the band and as their last year came to a close I started to listen to their music even more. As a tribute to one of the best boy bands of all time I decided to put together a few of my favorite songs from every album to share with new listeners! Have fun listening! - Maddy
